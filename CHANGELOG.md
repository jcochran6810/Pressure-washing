# Changelog

Customer-facing notes about what shipped, organized newest-first.
Edit this file with each release; the `/changelog` page renders it.

Categories:
- **New** — brand new feature you can try
- **Improved** — tweaks to existing features
- **Fixed** — bug fixes worth knowing about

---

## 2026-10-01

### New
- **Team seats.** Plus and Pro plans can now add employees at $5/user/month. Invite from Settings → Team.
- **Premium Templates add-on.** $5/month for Plus and Pro plans — pick which fields appear on the documents your customers see. Settings → Document customization.
- **"Prepared by" on estimates.** Sign your estimates with your name; appears on the PDF the customer sees.
- **Card required at signup.** Your card is captured upfront and charged automatically when the 14-day trial ends. Saves a step (no scrambling to add a card on day 14).

### Improved
- Pricing page now lists all available tiers in one place.
- Pre-deletion warning email goes out 7 days before a cancelled account's data is purged — with a one-click data export button.

## 2026-09-01

### New
- **Customer portal** at /portal — give your customers a passwordless login to see their invoices, estimates, and history.
- **Two-factor authentication** in Settings → Security.
- **Tax forms** page — Schedule C summary and 1099-NEC eligible vendors, CSV-downloadable.
- **Audit log** at /audit shows every important change anyone made in your account.

### Improved
- In-app notifications — bell icon in the top bar with unread count.

## 2026-08-01

### New
- **Stripe Connect** — payments from your customers go directly to your bank account.
- **Recurring contract billing** — convert a contract into a Stripe subscription so the customer is charged on schedule.
- **Inventory auto-deduct** — log chemicals used on a job; stock decrements automatically when the job is marked complete.
- **Edit estimates and invoices** after they're created (until accepted/paid).
- **Auto-save drafts** while you're filling out an estimate or invoice — never lose work to a closed tab.

## 2026-07-01

### New
- **Drag-and-drop calendar** to reschedule jobs.
- **Recurring contracts** auto-draft estimates and jobs on schedule.
- **SMS** via Telnyx for invoices, estimates, reminders, and waiver links.
- **QuickBooks Online** sync — push customers and invoices directly.
- **Liability waivers** with touch-signature on phone or tablet.
